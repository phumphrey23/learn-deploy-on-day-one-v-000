Biography

If I had to say, I’m a Designer/Marketer/Educator. My journey definitely makes me unique. I initially went to college for Mechanical Engineering but ended up graduating in Fashion Merchandising. Working for a small company in the fashion industry, I got a chance to work closely with the marketing team. Working for a smaller company, I was exposed to every part of the marketing team’s process; from brainstorming initiatives to deploying campaigns on multiple platforms. I eventually became their main graphic designer. I’ve always been big on education, so after working in the fashion industry, I got into teaching math, science, and electives related to my professional experience. Learning to code has always been on my to-do list and the Learn.co platform fits with my learning style. Any place where I can impart what I’ve learned with someone else who’s also on this journey is where I belong. Thanks to Flatiron School, I will become an awesome Developer/Designer/Marketer/Educator.
